## 📈 Strategic Guide: Surviving Week 1

To beat Week 1's **$300.00 Ledger Target**, follow these fundamental rules:

### Rule 1: The Discard Synergy Principle
Your cards have two values: **Hold Payout** (if kept in hand) and **Discard Impact** (drives price on discard). 
*   If you hold **CALL options**, discard **PUT options** to drive the stock price UP. This makes your Calls highly in-the-money!
*   If you hold **PUT options**, discard **CALL options** to drive the stock price DOWN. This leverages the Put multiplier for massive cash yields!

### Rule 2: Keep the Volatility Index Under Control
Every discard shifts volatility up. High volatility makes overnight GME shifts extremely random, meaning your stock price could collapse overnight.
*   Buy **Algorithmic Stabilizer** or **Volatility Hedge** in the shop.
*   Use stabilizer cards or discard stabilizer OTC contracts at the end of the day.

### Rule 3: Prune the Excess
Having too many puts in a CALL-heavy deck (or vice versa) ruins your hand draws.
*   Use the **Option Liquidation Desk** in the Shop to delete bad contracts permanently for $20.
*   During the Weekend Settle, always **Abandon** OTM options so they disappear from your deck for free.

### Rule 4: Buy Permanent Upgrades First
Cash in the shop should go to permanent shares:
*   **Leveraged Capital ($120):** Boosts base daily salary from $50 to $100. Payback period is just 3 days!
*   **Overnight Insurance ($90):** Prevents random overnight GME drops below -5%. Essential safety net.
